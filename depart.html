College Information

Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department offers state-of-the-art facilities for software development, research, and innovation. It provides a comprehensive curriculum covering topics like programming, algorithms, and data structures.

Electronics and Communication Department

The Electronics and Communication Department focuses on the design and development of electronic devices, communication systems, and signal processing. It offers modern labs and research opportunities.

Mechanical Engineering Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department provides education in the fields of mechanics, thermodynamics, and materials science. It has well-equipped labs and workshops for hands-on learning.

Civil Engineering Department

The Civil Engineering Department covers areas such as structural engineering, transportation, and environmental engineering. It offers practical experience through site visits and lab work.

Electrical Engineering Department

The Electrical Engineering Department focuses on power systems, electrical circuits, and control systems. It provides extensive lab facilities for students to gain practical knowledge.

Chemical Engineering Department

The Chemical Engineering Department deals with the design and operation of chemical processes. It offers courses in reaction engineering, process design, and material science.

Biotechnology Department

The Biotechnology Department explores the application of biological systems and organisms in technology. It provides research opportunities in genetics, bioinformatics, and molecular biology.
